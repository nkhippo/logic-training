# About This App

Logic Training is an app for systematically building the skills you need in business: reading, thinking, and writing logically.
Five tabs each focus on a different skill; using them together strengthens your overall logical ability.

## Role of Each Tab

| Tab | Main goal | Material |
|---|---|---|
| Fill-in | Read links between sentences accurately | Internal email, reports, proposals |
| Summarize | Compress information while keeping claims and evidence | Minutes, analysis reports, strategy documents |
| Critical Reading | Verify writing with “Can we really say that?” | Proposals, approval documents, recommendations |
| Sky-Rain-Umbrella | Derive interpretations from facts and plan next actions | Sales, marketing, management data |
| Consideration | Design writing that lands with the reader | Email, requests, proposals, self-introduction |

## Skill Phases

The five tabs map to three phases: **read → reason → write**.

**Read & understand** (Fill-in, Summarize)  
Train accurate grasp of structure, claims, and evidence.

**Reason & verify** (Critical Reading, Sky-Rain-Umbrella)  
Train spotting weak logic and deriving sound interpretation and action from facts.

**Write & communicate** (Consideration)  
Train designing messages the reader can act on, using reading and reasoning skills from other tabs.

## Suggested Use

If you are new, start with Fill-in at difficulty 1–2.
Once you are comfortable with how sentences connect, move on to Summarize and Critical Reading at higher difficulty.
Sky-Rain-Umbrella works well in parallel as practice in reasoning from business data.
Use Consideration as the “writing” capstone to apply what you built in the other tabs.
