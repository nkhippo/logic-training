# About Thinking Training

This training helps you define what to ask within fragmented, ambiguous situations,
and build the ability to guide judgment and action by stacking hypotheses.

## Six Thinking Types

| Type | What you do |
|---|---|
| ① Map the situation | Separate facts, uncertain information, and contradictions |
| ② Dig into causes | Go from surface causes down to root causes |
| ③ Look ahead | Estimate what could happen across multiple scenarios |
| ④ Compare options | Choose the best path using explicit criteria |
| ⑤ Move others | Design how to communicate and in what order |
| ⑥ Question the question | Check whether the question you are solving is the right one |

## Four Levels

- **Level 1**: Answer given questions to get a feel for each thinking type
- **Level 2**: Types are specified; you decide what to examine within each
- **Level 3**: You choose which types to use and in what order
- **Level 4**: Only a vague situation is given; start by defining the question

## About Scoring

Responses are scored internally, but scores are not shown to you.
The AI acts as a manager, asking follow-up questions without giving away the answer.

## About Reflection

After the final feedback, you may receive reflection prompts about your thinking process.
Answering is optional. You can respond up to two times.
